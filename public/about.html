What is Spotify Rewind?

Spotify Rewind is an application that allows users to see the songs and artists that they've listened to the most, across different periods of their listening journey.

Privacy

Your Spotify data is not sent to any extraneous server, nor do we save any of your data internally.

How do I switch accounts?

Then, open [xxx] and sign in with the desired account.
