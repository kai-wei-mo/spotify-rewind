What is Spotify Rewind?

Spotify Rewind is a simple way to track your favourite artists and songs. Rediscover the music you love and create playlists based on your listening habits today!

Privacy

Your Spotify data is not sent to any extraneous server, nor do we save any of your data internally.

How do I switch accounts?

Go to accounts.spotify.com and press LOG OUT.
